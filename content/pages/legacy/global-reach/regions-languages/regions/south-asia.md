## Praise and Prayer Needs for South Asia


We’re exploring the possibility of re-starting TTB’s ministry to Bangladesh in the Muslimi-Bengali language. Please pray that God would open the doors and clear the way for TTB to once again have a ministry of hope to this country.    


Pray for the hundreds of home listening groups meeting across India to listen to TTB together.


Pray for the many churches planted in Nepal by TTB listeners who gather together to hear the program and worship God. Pray also for those who experience opposition and persecution when they put their faith in Christ.


Pray for the distribution of TTB programs on Secure Digital (SD) cards in the Banjara and Gondi languages in India. Pray that God will lead TWR to the right partners to form home listening groups among these populations.


Alcohol abuse is a common problem in many parts of India. Pray that believers would come under personal conviction of this sin and turn their backs on it. Among those who are unsaved, pray that they will turn to faith in God, who is the only one who can provide the peace and comfort they seek.

#### Region Highlights


* North India is home to more than 100 of the world’s 639 unreached people groups.
* World mission experts tell us that 99.9 percent of the people living in Afghanistan and 99.2 percent of the people living in Pakistan are unreached.
* India’s population is almost 1.2 billion people – about 17% of the 7 billion people in the world today.
* India is also the largest English-speaking nation in the world, which is why we produce an English program in the Indian dialect.
* One-third of the population of Nepal lives on less than $1US a day, making it one of the poorest countries in the world.
