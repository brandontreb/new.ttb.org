## Praise and Prayer Needs


 Pray for the new Fongbe broadcasts that are now being heard throughout the birthplace of Voodoo in West Africa. Pray for the producer, Stanislas Ganse, that he would have divine wisdom as he translates and adapts these programs. Pray also for his protection as he leads this important ministry into the heart of one of Satan’s strongholds.  

  

Please pray that God would both guide and provide as we consider additional ministry opportunities to the people of Africa. There is a great need for sound Bible instruction. If it’s in God’s will to increase TTB’s African ministry, we want to be obedient and faithful in doing so!
