## Praise and Prayer Needs


 Praise God that the newly-produced German programs successfully went back on the air on April 1, 2013. Pray they will be used by God to get people deeper into His Word. Also pray for German-speaking people all over the world who will be listening via the Internet.   

  

Please pray for our new German producer, Rev. Woytschak, as he undertakes the great work of voicing TTB and sharing Dr. McGee’s teaching of the whole Word with his countrymen.

We are heartily bound together in the love of Jesus.
